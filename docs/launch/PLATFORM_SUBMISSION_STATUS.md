### Long Description
Polycopy is a copy-trading platform for Polymarket that lets users follow and automatically replicate trades from top-performing traders. 

**Key Features:**
- Follow 500K+ Polymarket traders
- Real-time trade copying with detailed analytics
- Non-custodial - you control your keys
- Auto-close positions when original trader exits
- Portfolio tracking and PnL analytics
- Free tier with manual copying
- Premium tier ($20/mo) with automated execution

**Who It's For:**
- Polymarket users looking to improve returns
- Crypto and prediction market traders
- Anyone who wants to follow "smart money"
- Both beginners and experienced traders

**Tech Stack:** Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase, Stripe
