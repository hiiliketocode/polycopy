I'll be honest - I was terrible at Polymarket trading. Not just "having a bad month" terrible, but genuinely bad. I'd chase trending markets, make emotional bets, and generally do everything wrong. Sound familiar?

**The "Copy Everything" Problem**

Most copy trading tools take the nuclear approach: pick a trader, copy EVERYTHING they do. But this never felt right to me. Here's why:

Even the best traders make moves I don't understand. They take positions I'm not comfortable with. They have different risk tolerance, different market knowledge, different timeframes. Blindly copying everything isn't learning - it's just delegation with extra steps.

**What I Actually Wanted**

I wanted something different: **curation, not automation**.

I wanted to see what top traders were doing in real-time, but maintain the agency to choose which trades made sense for me. I wanted to learn from their moves while still thinking for myself.

So I built Polycopy.

**How It Works**

Think of Polycopy like a Twitter feed, but for Polymarket trades:

1. **Follow traders you trust** - Search and filter 500K+ traders by ROI, win rate, trading history, and category expertise
2. **Get a curated feed** - See their trades as they happen in a clean, chronological feed
3. **Pick and choose** - Decide which trades align with your thesis and risk tolerance
4. **Execute easily** - Copy trades manually (free) or with one-click execution (premium)

**The Key Insight**

The magic isn't in the automation - it's in the curation. You're not blindly following anyone. You're creating your own personalized feed of opportunities from proven traders, then making informed decisions.

The best part? I'm still thinking for myself. I'm just doing it with better information.
